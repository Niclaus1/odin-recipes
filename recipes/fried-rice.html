Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This 15-minute fried rice tastes just like restaurant-style!

Just use leftover rice, eggs, carrots, peas, and soy sauce.

Garnish with green onions if desired.

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup chopped baby carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen green peas
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups leftover cooked and chilled white rice
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce, to taste
  • 2 tsp sesame oil, to taste

Steps

  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Boil Carrots for 5 minutes, add peas, then drain
  3. Stir-fry, heat oil in a wok add carrots, peas, and garlic, cook for 30 mins
  4. Scramble Eggs, Add eggs ans stir quickly to scramble with vegetables
  5. Add Rice, Stir in cooked rice, add soy sauce, and toss to coat
  6. Drizzle with sesame oil, toss again
  7. Serve Hot!r

Nutritional Fact per serving

  • 152 Calories
  • 7g Fat
  • 19g Carbs
  • 4g Protein
