Cordon Bleu Recipe

Description

Cordon bleu is traditionally meat wrapped around cheese, breaded, and fried. This modern version is baked.

Classic chicken cordon bleu uses chicken cutlets, ham, Swiss cheese, bread crumbs, and spices, topped with a creamy Dijon sauce.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Quick Melt Cheese
  • Cooked Ham
  • Seasoned Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Gather all Ingredients
  2. Pound the Chicken Breast
  3. Sprinkle each piece of chicken on both sides with salt and pepper
  4. Place 1 Cheese and 1 Sliced ham on top of each breast
  5. Roll up each breast and secure with toothpick
  6. Placed in the prepared baking dish and sprinkle chicken evenly with bread crumbs
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink, 30-35 minutes
  8. Remove from the oven, and place Cheese Slice on top of each breast
  9. Return to the oven until cheese has melted, 5 minutes
  10. Remove toothpick and Serve!

Nutritional Fact per serving

  • 19g Fat

  • 46g Protein
