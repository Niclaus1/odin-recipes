Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description

Chicken katsu is a Japanese fried chicken dish. This family recipe also works for tonkatsu using pork cutlets.

Serve with rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken Breast Halves
  • Salt and Pepper
  • 2 tbsp All-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • Frying Oil

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper.
  2. Dredge in flour, dip in egg, and coat with panko.
  3. Heat oil over medium-high heat and fry chicken until golden, 4 minutes per side.
  4. Drain on paper towels.

Nutritional Facts per serving

  • 297 Calories
  • 11g Fat
  • 22g Carbs
  • 31g Protein
